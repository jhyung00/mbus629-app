Key Accounting Formulas

ROE: Return on Equity Net Income / Average Equity
ROA: Return on Assets Net Income / Average Assets
Financial Leverage Average Assets / Average Equity
Profit Margin Net Income / Sales
Asset Turnover Sales / Average Assets
NOA: Net Operating Assets Operating assets - Operating liabilities
NOPBT: Net Operating Profit Before Tax Revenue - Operating expenses including COGS
NNE: Net Nonoperating Expense After Tax Pretax net nonoperating expense × (1 - Tax%)
NOPAT: Net Operating Profit After Tax
1. NOPBT - Tax on operating profit
2. Net income + NNE
RNOA: Return on Net Operating Assets NOPAT / Average Net Operating Assets
Accounts Receivable Turnover Net Sales / Average Accounts Receivable
Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) 365 / Accounts Receivable Turnover
PP&E Turnover Sales / Average PP&E, Net
LIFO Reserve Impact FIFO Inventory = LIFO Inventory + LIFO Reserve
Cumulative Tax Savings = LIFO Reserve × Tax Rate
Average Useful Life & Percent Used Up Avg Useful Life = Depreciable Asset Cost / Depreciation Exp
% Used Up = Accumulated Depreciation / Depreciable Asset Cost
